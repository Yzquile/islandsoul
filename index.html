Island Soul Siquijor

Discover the Magic of Siquijor Island

Welcome to Paradise

Nestled in the heart of the Visayan archipelago in the Philippines, Siquijor is a captivating island that boasts a rich history steeped in mysticism and cultural diversity. Believed to have been inhabited by the indigenous people long before the Spanish colonization, Siquijor became a focal point for trade and cultural exchange. The island, once known as "Isla del Fuego" or Island of Fire, derived its name from the eerie glow produced by the numerous fireflies that illuminated its shores at night. During the Spanish era, the island became a haven for healers and mystical practices, earning a reputation for traditional folk medicine and spiritual rituals. Today, Siquijor welcomes visitors with open arms, inviting them to explore its pristine beaches, lush landscapes, and delve into the echoes of its intriguing past, where the mystique of the island continues to captivate the curious traveler.
